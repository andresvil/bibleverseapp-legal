  ## Common questions                                       

  **How do I restore my purchase on a new device?**                                                                 
  Open the app → **Profile** tab → **Restore purchases**. As long as you're signed in to the same Apple ID, your
  premium access will come back.                                                                                    
                                                            
  **How do I cancel my subscription?**                                                                              
  Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone: **Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Memorize Bible 
  Verses By Heart → Cancel Subscription.**                                                                          
   
  **My streak disappeared / a verse is in the wrong stage.**                                                        
  Email us with your app version and a brief description. We may ask for a screenshot.
                                                                                                                    
  **Which Bible translations do you use?**                                                                          
  We currently use the **World English Bible (WEB)** in English and **Reina-Valera 1909** in Spanish — both are in  
  the public domain.                                                                                                
                                                            
  **Can I request a translation, theme, or feature?**                                                               
  Yes — please email us. We prioritize requests we hear about most often.
